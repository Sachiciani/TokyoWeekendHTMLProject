Tokyo for a Weekend

Tokyo is the capital of Japan. It is a popular destination for tourists and locals alike. Here is my top 5 neighborhoods in Tokyo:

  1. Shibuya

  2. Harajuku

  3. Shinjuku

  4. Shimokitazawa

  5. Kichijoji

If you haven't noticed it yet, I am a big fan of the Western side of Tokyo. Although it is my preference, there are still so many cool places on the East side.
